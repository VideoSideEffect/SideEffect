ABOUT

Visual studio Side Effect

Side Effect Studio was founded in 2012. by Raitis. Since then, the studio has produced original audio video commercials, music videos, feature films, and has worked creatively in 3D visioning for commercial and artistic projects within the audiovisual industry. In Studio Side Effect Raitis as a leading artist is collaborating with other professional editors and artists. Each team member has been invited to be a part of this studio, because of his professionalism in specific area, personal dedication and ability to work in this team, to achieve the best results for our clients and partners.

Studio Side Effect develops visual material for a variety of purposes

We will help you to:
tell your story in professional visual communication
raise visibility of your project, brand, music, movie by creating recognizable imagery and authentic visual identity
explain your idea by creating visual story as a video or graphic artwork
